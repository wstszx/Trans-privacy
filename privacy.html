TransText Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how TransText ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is handled.

Information Collection

TransText collects and stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • Translation history (your recent translations)
  • Language preferences (your selected interface language)
  • User interface settings

All this information is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is not transmitted to our servers.

Translation Service

When you use TransText to translate text:

  • The text you want to translate is sent to our translation API service
  • The translation is processed and returned to your browser
  • No personal information is attached to translation requests
  • We do not store your translated text on our servers

Data Storage

TransText stores data in the following ways:

  • Local Storage: Translation history and settings are stored locally in your browser
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any user data on our servers
  • Data Retention: Local data remains until you clear it or uninstall the extension

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All API communications are encrypted using HTTPS
  • Translation requests are authenticated using secure API tokens
  • No personal identification information is collected or transmitted

User Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Clear History: You can delete your translation history at any time
  • Data Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible to you
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension will delete all stored local data

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
